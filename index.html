Phonedrone is our revolutionary software which enables your drone to seek-and-locate your mobile phone, and transmit its location to your PC or smartphone. With "The Claw" add on, your drone can even carry your phone back to you - wherever you are!

Phonedrone learns the layout of your home, apartment or workplace, as you walk it thorugh and teach it the names for each room or area. It also learns to recognise your most used possessions, as you tag and name them in our special software. Phonedrone can then fly round, seek-and-locating these possessions, transmitting their location back to your PC or smartphone, and with "The Claw" addon returning them back to your hand.
